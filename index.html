Welcome!

I'm Luke Belcourt! I'm a writer of LGBT adventure fantasy novels, born and raised in Glasgow, Scotland.

My latest series, Glasgow Witches, is out now, beginning with Witches of Merchant City, and following up with Vampires in Park Circus.

Outside of writing, I'm also a fan of tabletop RPGs, and have a life-ruining Pokémon addiction. Please show me your cool Pokémon, I need to see them.
