Have you ever forgotten something important?

Holly Winter is jaded, disgruntled and recently unemployed. There's something missing from her life.

When her flatmate vanishes in a home-made magical ritual, she starts to remember what she's lost.

Uncovering magic she'd buried away will have Holly using the Glasgow Underground as a banishing circle to send a demon back to hell.

She'll fight with her coven against the Witch Queen of Elf-hame.

She'll follow a path she's felt on the periphery of her whole life.

The path of the witch.

But to uncover those memories, Holly needs the help of a little bookshop in the middle of Trongate.

She needs the Coven of Merchant City.
